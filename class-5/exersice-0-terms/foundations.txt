What are data types?
    Number, string, boolean, NULL, undefined, and symbol.

What are operators used for?
    To assign values, compare values, preform operations, and more.

What are conditional statements?
    Represented in the form of "if...then".

What are loops?
    Loops like "for" or "while" go through blocks of code a number of times or go through a block of code while a specified condition is true.
